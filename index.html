About Me#

Yongwei Nie is now an Associate Professor at the School of Computer Science and Engineering of South China University of Technology, Guangzhou, Guangdong, China. He received his doctor and bachelor degree from the Computer School of Wuhan University in 2015 and 2009, respectively. His research interests lie in Computer Graphics and Computer Vision, and he is now more focused on multimodal large language models, video understanding, and multi-agent systems.

📧 Contact: nieyongwei@scut.edu.cn | Google Scholar | DBLP
